About Ashirwad Typing Institute

Who We Are

Ashirwad Typing Institute is a leading professional training center dedicated to imparting quality education in Marathi and English typing. With years of experience and a team of expert instructors, we have helped hundreds of students master typing skills and build successful careers.

Our Mission

To provide world-class typing education that empowers students with practical skills, professional development, and career advancement opportunities in the digital age.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred typing institute, recognized for excellence in education and student success, creating a community of skilled typists who contribute to the digital economy.
